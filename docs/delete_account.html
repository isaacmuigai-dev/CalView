Delete Your CalViewAI Account

Last updated: January 08, 2026

We respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your CalViewAI account and associated data.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly within the CalViewAI app by following these steps:

  1. Open the CalViewAI app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Settings tab (gear icon).
  3. Scroll down to the "Account" section.
  4. Tap on "Delete Account".
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted.

Your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately.

Alternative Method: Contact Us

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at:

Email: support@calviewai.com

Please include the email address associated with your CalViewAI account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data is Deleted?

Data Type Status Details
Account Information Deleted Immediately Name, email, profile picture
Personal Details Deleted Immediately Height, weight, age, gender, goals
Meal History Deleted Immediately All logged meals, food scans, and nutrition data
Health Data Deleted Immediately Steps, calories burned, weight history, progress data
Preferences & Settings Deleted Immediately Notification settings, theme preferences, language
Anonymous Analytics Retained (Anonymized) Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics may be retained for service improvement

Data Retention Period

  • Cloud Data (Firebase): Deleted immediately upon account deletion.
  • Local Data: Deleted from your device immediately. If you reinstall the app, no previous data will be recovered.
  • Backup Systems: Data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 30 days before being permanently purged.

Third-Party Data

CalViewAI integrates with the following third-party services. Deleting your CalViewAI account does not automatically delete data from these services:

  • Google Account: Your Google account remains unaffected. You can manage your Google data at myaccount.google.com.
  • Health Connect: Data synced to Health Connect is managed by Android. You can delete this data in your device's Health Connect settings.
